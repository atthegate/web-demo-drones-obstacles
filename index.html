FAA Obstacles

    FAA Obstacles Map - Oregon

    What? A map of structures listed in the FAA's Digital Obstacle File (DOF) for the state of Oregon. The DOF is a publicly available database of "all known obstacles of interest to aviation users in the United States", specifically all structures over 200 feet tall or any other structures that could affect navigable airspace.

    Why? The purpose of this demo is to show the locations and relevant information of structures considered to be aeronautical obstacles in the FAA's DOF database. This data can be used to help inform UAS flight planning by adding awareness of obstacles that could impact safe and successful operations.

    How? Click on the X to close this window. Clicking on an obstacle marker will open a pop-up with more information about the selected obstacle. Also, you can filter by obstacle type using the icons on the left.
